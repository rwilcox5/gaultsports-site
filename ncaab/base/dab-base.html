How it Works

Every team should have an equal opportunity to prove themselves. A team that plays an easy schedule should not be dismissed but simply forced to win games by more impressive margins. And a team that regularly loses to top competition should be rewarded for keeping games close.

DAB works by determining how a typical bubble team would perform against each team's schedule. This expected performance is compared to the actual performance and if the team is usually doing better than an expected bubble team they should be in, and if they usually underperform then they should be out.
